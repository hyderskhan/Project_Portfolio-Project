About Me

Hi! My name is Hyder Khan and welcome to my project portfolio! I am eager to show you all some of projects I worked on my first few weeks of learning coding but before that let me share a bit more about myself. I have always been interested in learning web development and have had opportunities in the past but fate always took me the other way (maybe it was for this day to come).

After graduating college with a BBA in Finance from Baruch College, I joined a real estate start-up, WeWork. The four years there, I spent my time between the Supply Chain/Procurement and Cost Management team for multiple business units. A year into COVID, I decided to move on and take on another challenge which led me to a sustainable e-commerce start-up, Public Goods. I joined as their FP&A Manager and took charge of business forecasting and performance analysis along with many other responsibilities (funny enough QA'ing our website became one of them).

After a year at Public Goods, I made a tough decision to leave and take a break in my career. A month into my break, I kept thinking about what I wanted to do next. A good friend for years kept telling me how they thought I should join the world of tech. I had thought about it myself but never had the time, until now. I began reading and watching whatever resources I could find online to see if this is the path I wanted to start on. I was sold immediately. I enjoy the problem solving, critical thinking and creative aspect that comes with coding.

After doing research on where I wanted to learn how to code (bootcamp, self-taught, etc.), I decided to enroll in Codecademy's Full-Stack Engineer course. It's been a great journey so far, I have acquired new skills and completed many projects that you can find below.
